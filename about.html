Who Are We?

The Bissett Consulting Organization (BCO) is a student-run club under the Students' Association of Mount Royal University (SAMRU), founded in September 2025 to give MRU students meaningful exposure to the world of consulting and strategy through hands-on events, case competitions, and industry connections.

Our first year exceeded expectations. We grew to 30 members, ran a full slate of programming, and built real recognition across campus. What started as an idea became a community, and that community is now ready to grow. Demand for consulting education at MRU is strong, and BCO is positioned to meet it.

In 2026–2027, we're expanding our events, deepening our industry partnerships, and evolving our programming to reflect the consulting landscape students will actually enter — including the growing role of AI in strategy and how firms deliver value. Our programming this year includes Intro to Consulting, AI and Consulting, Case Night, Day in Consulting, and the BCO Consulting Cup.

BCO is open to students from every faculty at Mount Royal University. We believe diverse perspectives produce better thinking, and we're committed to building a space where students from business, STEM, arts, and beyond can develop the analytical, communication, and problem-solving skills that define great consultants.

Our 2026–2027 events

Five programs this year, each designed to build a core consulting skill.

Introduction to Consulting

BCO's opening event of the year, open to all MRU students. This session breaks down what consulting actually is, the types of firms and roles that exist, the skills that matter most, and what a career in strategy looks like in practice. Attendees leave with a clearer picture of the industry and a direct connection to BCO and its programming.

Tentative Date: September 2026 · On-Campus

AI and Consulting

A BCO-exclusive event exploring how artificial intelligence is reshaping the strategy and consulting landscape. Led by a partner firm, members get practical exposure to how AI is actually being used in consulting engagements today — from research automation to decision-support tools — and what that means for students entering the industry.

Tentative Date: October 2026 · On-Campus

Case Night

Exclusive to BCO members, Case Night is an interactive workshop that introduces the fundamental frameworks used by top consulting firms, with an emphasis on structured problem-solving, analytical thinking, and client communication. Members walk away better prepared for actual interviews, competitions, and internship placements.

Tentative Dates: November 2026 & March 2027 · On-Campus

Day in Consulting

A two-hour immersive event bringing students directly into the offices of a partner firm for an unfiltered look at life as a consultant — a guided office tour, a firm-led skills workshop, a structured Q&A with practicing consultants, and informal networking with firm representatives.

Tentative Date: January 2027 · Partner Firm Offices or On-Campus

BCO Consulting Cup

Our flagship case competition, open campus-wide. Teams work under time constraints to analyze a real business case, develop strategic recommendations, and present solutions to an industry judging panel — open to students of all backgrounds who want to challenge themselves and build real-world problem-solving skills.
